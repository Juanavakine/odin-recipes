Pico De Gallo


Lets get fresh with it

Pico de Gallo made fresh is the best way. Makes an amazing topping for mexican dishes and taste amazing on its own or with tortilla chips! Come give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup onion(finely chopped)
  • 1 medium jalapeno(finely chopped)
  • 1/4cup lime juice
  • 3/4th tsp salt
  • 1.5 lbs ripe red tomatoes(finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cilantro(chopped)

Steps

  1. Mix onion, jalapeno, lime, juice, salt.
  2. Let it sit for 5min
  3. Add tomatoes, taste for salt
  4. At this point the pico is ready to eat but taste best when its settled for 10 minutes

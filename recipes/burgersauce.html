Burger Sauce


The Sauce itself

Popular condiment, made from other condiments

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayo
  • 3 tbps ketchup
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbps dill pickle
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp onion powder
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk all ingredients until fully incorporated
  2. store in airtight container until ready to use
